Malware Analysis Trainings Samples

Samplepedia is a searchable collection of malware analysis training samples for security researchers, students, and professionals who want to improve their analysis skills through self-paced learning. Educators can find samples for their trainings that match their particular goals. Learners can submit samples or solutions and compare approaches with others in the community.

Why Samplepedia?

I created this application because I often need to find samples that can teach very specific things. At best I remember that there was a sample some years ago that did exactly what I need, but I can never find it again. Or worse -- I started to watch my own Youtube videos to figure out what I did there. Furthermore, I cannot count the number of times people ask me where they can find easy samples to practice malware analysis. This should hopefully solve this problem.
